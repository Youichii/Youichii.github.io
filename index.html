Hackathon - Space Office

21/02/2021 Louvain-La-Neuve

We decided to participate in a hackathon to test our skills and learn new things. We had a lot of fun and we learned a lot. The theme was Space Office, a project that aims to create an application based on space. We developped a 2D shooter game with Python but there was a twist. The only way to make the spaceship move was by moving our retinas. The webcam of our laptop was used to detect the movement of our eyes. Blinking was used to make the Space Ship fire. So we spent a entiere week-end working on that project and we finished in the 2nd place exaequo.
